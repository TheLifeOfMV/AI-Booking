Visual Style

The application features a modern, clean, and user-friendly design aesthetic. It prioritizes clarity and ease of use, typical for health or service-booking apps with minimal clutter, professional appearance, approachable elements, and clear visual hierarchy.

Textures & Patterns

The app uses subtle gradients and decorative patterns:

  • Subtle light blue/grey radial or linear gradients for backgrounds
  • Decorative wavy line patterns (in dark grey/black) for visual interest

Typography

A clean, legible sans-serif typeface is used throughout the application (SF Pro or similar alternative).

Large Title (Bold, 32px)

Used for main screen titles and hero content.

Section Heading (Semibold, 28px)

Used for major sections like "Specialty", "Doctors", "Upcoming".

Card Title (Semibold, 20px)

Used for card titles like doctor names "Vinny Vang", "Eleanor Padilla".

Body Text (Regular, 16px)

Used for most content like "Female, 25 y.o", "10+ years experienced".

Small/Caption Text (Regular, 14px)

Used for secondary information like dates/times on past appointments.

Typography Guidelines

  • Hierarchy: Achieved through variations in size and weight.
  • Font Weight: Bold (700) for emphasis, Semibold (600) for headings, Regular (400) for body text.
  • Line Height: 1.5 for body text, slightly tighter for headings.
  • Case: Sentence case and Title Case are used appropriately.
  • Alignment: Primarily left-aligned text for readability. Centered text used sparingly for headings or emphasis.

Button Guidelines

  • Primary CTAs: Large, solid blue background, white text, rounded corners, often include a right-facing arrow icon.
  • Secondary/Selection Buttons: Smaller buttons with light grey backgrounds, dark grey text, and rounded corners.
  • Toggle Buttons: Simple text buttons grouped together. The selected option has a subtle light grey background highlight.
  • Text Buttons: Used for less prominent actions like "See all". Typically plain text in the primary blue.
  • Small Action Buttons: Black background, white text, rounded pill shape for price tags.

Card Guidelines

  • Style: Rounded corners (8px radius), white or dark backgrounds, and subtle drop shadows.
  • Content: Combine images/avatars, text (various hierarchy levels), icons, and sometimes buttons.
  • Interaction: Cards are typically clickable/tappable to navigate to detail screens.
  • Elevation: Subtle shadows create a sense of depth and hierarchy in the interface.

Layout

The application uses a structured layout approach to organize content effectively.

Structure

  • Vertical scrolling lists dominate
  • Content is organized into clear sections using headings and cards
  • Consistent use of padding and margins creates a breathable layout
  • Primary screens follow a clear visual hierarchy
